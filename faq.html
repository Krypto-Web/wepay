Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn on WePay?

View ads or surf sponsored sites, stay for the required timer, then claim your reward. New items become available again after the daily reset at midnight.

How do I get paid?

Request a withdrawal from the Withdraw page with your USDT wallet address and network (TRC20, ERC20, or BEP20). Our team reviews and sends withdrawals manually, usually within 24–48 hours.

Is there a minimum withdrawal amount?

Check the current minimum on the Withdraw page. There are no upfront fees to request a withdrawal.

What happens if my withdrawal is rejected?

If a withdrawal is rejected (for example, an invalid wallet address), the full amount is automatically refunded to your wallet balance.

How does the referral program work?

Share your unique referral link from the Referrals page. When someone signs up through it, you earn a one-time referral bonus.

Why was my ad/surf session cancelled?

Sessions are time-verified on our server. If you return and claim before the required time has passed, the claim will be rejected — simply wait for the full timer next time.
